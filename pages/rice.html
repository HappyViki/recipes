Rice

Total Time: 25 min

Steps

  1. Boil water in pot on high heat
  2. Add rice and cover with lid
  3. Wait for it to bubble
  4. Turn down heat to lowest setting
  5. Simmer until water is absorbed, usually 15-20 minutes
  6. Fork, cover, and wait for another 5 minutes
  7. Optional: pan fry in fat from meat you just made
  8. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati or jasmine rice
  • 2 cup water
