Cookie Base

Oven: 350f

Bake: 13 min

Steps

  1. Slightly melt butter in frying pan
  2. Move pan off heat
  3. Add sugar, vanilla, and egg (make sure pan is not that hot before adding egg)
  4. Mix well with spatula
  5. Add flour, baking soda, salt
  6. Mix with spatula, then finish mixing with hands
  7. Mix in toppings
  8. Form into balls and put on baking sheet with a bit of space between
  9. Press down on each ball until flat
  10. Bake and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 6 tbsp melted salted butter
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 egg
  • 1 1/2 cup flour
  • 3/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • any toppings!
