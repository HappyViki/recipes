Burger

Total Time: 25 min

Steps

  1. Heat pan on medium heat with butter
  2. Brown burger buns in pan and set aside
  3. Sizzle the burgers on one side until brown, 1-3 minutes
  4. Turn down heat to medium-low if it's sizzling too much, and up to medium if not enough
  5. Flip burgers and cover with lid
  6. Flip every 5 minutes, press with spatula a little
  7. Ready after pink stuff came out and sizzling on top after flipping on both sides
  8. Optional: caramelize something in the fat for later
  9. Assemble and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1-4 burgers
  • 1-4 Dave's Killer Bread Burger Buns Done Right
  • 1 dab butter
  • Optional toppings: salt, ketchup, cabbage, montreal seasoning, etc.
