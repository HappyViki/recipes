Steak

Husband's job :)

Oven: 375f

Bake: 13 min

Total Time: 19 min

Steps

  1. Heat pan on medium heat with olive oil and butter
  2. Season well
  3. Sear well on each side for 3 minutes
  4. Bake and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 4 dashes of seasoning
  • 3 dabs butter and olive oil
  • 2 ny strip or t-bone steaks
