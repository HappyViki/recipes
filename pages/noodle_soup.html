Noodle Soup

Total Time: 15 min

Steps

  1. Make noodles
  2. Heat pan on medium heat with butter
  3. Caramelize veggies and nuts
  4. Add meat, noodles, and broth and wait until hot
  5. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 portions Delallo Capellini or De Cecco Fusilli
  • 2 portions broth
  • 2 portions leftover meat
  • Veggie options: carrots, zucchini, yellow squash, onion, etc
  • Nut options: walnuts, pecans, hazelnuts, etc
