Stew Meat

Time: 2 hr

Steps

  1. Put stew meat in frying pan
  2. Season with salt
  3. Optional: season with favorite seasonings
  4. Stir on medium heat until all sides are brown
  5. Turn down heat to lowest heat setting and cover with lid
  6. Wait and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds stew meat
  • 3 dashes salt
  • Seasoning options: black pepper, umami mushroom seasoning, bbq seasoning, etc.
