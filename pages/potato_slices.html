Potato Slices

Total Time: 25 min

Steps

  1. Heat pan on medium heat with oil
  2. Chop potato thin and even
  3. Stir in pan until coated in oil, you may need to add more oil
  4. Turn down heat to medium-low heat, watch heat, you may need to adjust to slightly sizzle
  5. Stir until starting to caramelize
  6. Cover with lid
  7. Flip slices every 5 minutes
  8. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 3 dabs butter and/or olive oil
  • 3 yukon gold potatos or 2 russet potatos
  • Seasoning options: black pepper, montreal steak seasoning, etc.
