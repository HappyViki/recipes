Beef Ribs

Oven: 375f

Bake: 15 min

Total Time: 21 min

Steps

  1. Heat frying pan on medium heat with oils until hot
  2. Optional: season with favorite seasonings
  3. Sear ribs for 3 minutes, repeat on other side
  4. Transfer to baking sheet
  5. Bake and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless beef ribs
  • 1 dab butter
  • 1 dash olive oil
  • Seasoning options: black pepper, umami mushroom seasoning, bbq seasoning, etc.
