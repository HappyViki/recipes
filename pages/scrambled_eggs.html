Scrambled Eggs

Time: 7 min

Steps

  1. Heat pan on medium heat with oil (and sprinkle in optional seasoning)
  2. Scramble eggs
  3. Wait until pan is hot
  4. Sizzle the eggs and slowly move them around
  5. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 dab butter or olive oil
  • Seasoning options: cinnamon, cardamom, oregano, etc.
