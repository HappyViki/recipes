Stir Fry

Total Time: 15-30 min (depending on what I add)

Steps

  1. Heat pan on medium heat with oil
  2. Cut up optional veggies, nuts, and leftover meat
  3. Pan fry things in order of time needed to cook thoroughly
  4. Nuts, then veggies, then meat, then base
  5. Caramelize at each step as much as possible
  6. Make sure the food is only slightly sizzling, not too much
  7. If food is sticking to pan: add more oil and lower heat
  8. If nuts/veggies look done: set aside before adding meat
  9. Mix and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 3 dabs butter and/or olive oil
  • 2 portions leftover meat: beef, chicken
  • 2 portions base: rice, potato, noodles, bread
  • Veggie options: carrots, zucchini, yellow squash, onion, etc
  • Nut options: walnuts, pecans, hazelnuts, etc
