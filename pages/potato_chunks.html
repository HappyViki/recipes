Potato Chunks

Oven: 425f

Bake: 45 min

Steps

  1. Cut up potatoes into chunks.
  2. Put on baking sheet.
  3. Drizzle olive oil and sprinkle seasoning.
  4. Mix well with hands.
  5. Bake and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 3 drizzles olive oil
  • 2 lbs medium size potatos
  • Seasoning options: rosemary, thyme, sea salt, black pepper, etc.
